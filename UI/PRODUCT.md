# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Investors seeking unbiased, data-grounded insights into Indian mutual funds. They need a tool to strip away marketing hype and view actual returns, expense ratios, portfolio allocations, and tax liabilities.

## Product Purpose
FundScope is a precision mutual fund analytics dashboard. It scores funds 1-100 (Grades S to D) based on historical CAGR, volatility, risk-adjusted ratios, and consistency, and provides tracking, comparison, and planning tools.

## Brand Personality
- Precision: Analytical, exact, and raw (no decorative shadows or fluff).
- Premium: Sleek dark theme, high-contrast accents, refined serif typography, and tactile feedback.
- Delightful: Fluid spring animations and physical micro-interactions.

## Anti-references
- AcmeVenture / generic SaaS templates with purple gradients and rounded white boxes.
- Emojis in code or copy.
- Giant shouting headers and meta-labels like "SECTION 01".

## Design Principles
- Data First: Monospace font for numbers, clear visual hierarchy, and zero clutter.
- Organic Tactility: Everything interactive reacts with spring physics and scale changes.
- Truthful Copy: Concrete financial terminology, clear labels, and helpful guides.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` with crossfades.
- Maintain high contrast on text.
